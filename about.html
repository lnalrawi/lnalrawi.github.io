ABOUT ME

As a professional, I am a Global Technology Engineer at Parker Wellbore, where I drive digital innovation and technology transformation in the oil and gas industry.

As a project leader I have successfully managed and delivered multiple international projects with budgets ranging from $100K to $400K. These projects focused on implementing advanced technology solutions, streamlining operations, and addressing complex industry challenges across diverse regions.

As a scientific researcher, As a scientific researcher, I focus on the intersection of Human-Centered Computing and Energy Informatics. Within Human-Centered Computing, I focus on interaction design and visual communication for 2D and 3D user interfaces in immersive technologies, including Extended reality and Haptics Feedback technology. In Energy Informatics, my work emphasizes human factors, exploring the critical role of human interaction in optimizing energy systems and technologies, ultimately improving oil and gas operations and safety. Additionally, I contribute to the academic community as a reviewer for leading HCI conferences and journals, including CHI, IEEE VR, TEI, AsiaHaptics, OzCHI, and SUI.

As a passionate speaker, I inspire audiences by sharing insights on digital innovation, interaction design, and the transformative power of technology in the oil and gas industry and beyond. My talks bridge industry expertise with cutting-edge research in augmented and virtual reality.


Driving the adoption of cutting-edge technologies—such as immersive systems, interaction design, and energy informatics—to solve complex industry challenges, optimize workflows, and shape the future of industrial digitalization. Through research, innovation, and industry collaboration, I bridge the gap between technology and real-world applications, ensuring that advancements in AR/VR, haptics, and AI-driven interfaces are both groundbreaking and practical.


I envision a future where immersive and intelligent technologies revolutionize industrial operations, creating safer, smarter, and more efficient workplaces. By continuously advancing human-computer interaction and digital transformation, I aim to contribute to an industry where technology seamlessly empowers professionals, minimizes risks, and maximizes productivity, ultimately shaping a more sustainable and innovative global energy sector.
